# Curation Guide

This guide describes how content should be selected, grouped, and improved.

## Selection Principles

Good sources are usually:

- long-form enough to contain durable context;
- relevant to AI research, AI product work, LLM engineering, agents, AI coding, AI startups, or creative tooling;
- attributable through a public source URL;
- useful for readers who want to revisit the material later.

## Metadata Standards

- Use the creator, podcast, or publishing channel as `channel`.
- Keep the original source URL.
- Prefer the original publish date over the import date.
- Use consistent channel naming so archive pages stay stable.
- Keep public records free of private Notion links.

## Topic Index Direction

Topic pages should be useful learning paths, not just tag dumps.

Candidate topics:

- AI agents
- LLM engineering
- AI product management
- AI coding
- AI startups
- model research
- evaluation and reliability
- creative AI tooling
